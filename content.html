About

Avignone's was established in 1971 and has been enjoyed by locals for a very long time with a amazing history! An intimate, relaxing venue to meet your friends and make new ones. Everyone welcome! Cheers to having a great time!

We have Dodger Games, DJ that spins Friday night, Karaoke and more.
